YOU CAN COOK
is a celebration of food,
people and cultures!

We've been going over 10 years

Celebrating our 10th anniversary in 2017

YOU CAN COOK organises cookery classes, demonstrations and workshops on nutrition and food related issues all over Scotland.

YOU CAN COOK was set up by entrepreneur Bosco Santimano,
as a means of helping local and national communities lead healthier lives on a budget.
